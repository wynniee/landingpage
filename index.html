Our History

Founded in 2022, UrbanLuxe was born out of a deep passion for bags and a desire to blend urban sophistication with luxury. Our founder, Mrs. Q, has always had an eye for fashion and a special love for bags that not only serve as practical accessories but also as statements of style and elegance.
At UrbanLuxe, we believe that a great bag can transform your outfit and boost your confidence. Our mission is to provide bags that are not only fashionable but also versatile, catering to the needs of the modern, on-the-go individual.

Join us on this journey to redefine urban elegance and discover the perfect bag to elevate your style!
